Dashboard Help

  • Add Widgets: Open any page, click extension icon, select "Start Selection".
  • Edit Mode: Click "Edit Layout" to enable moving and resizing.
  • Move: Drag the widget header.
  • Resize: Drag the bottom-right corner (snaps to 10px).
  • Zoom: Use + / - buttons on the widget.
  • Undo: Press Ctrl + Z (or Cmd + Z) to undo actions (max 5 steps).
  • Bring to Front: Click any widget to bring it to the top.
  • Delete: Click the × button on the widget header.
  • Invert: Click the ◑ button to toggle dark mode for a widget.
